ABOUT

Currently, I work as a Data Visualization Developer on Boundary Remote Subsurface Solutions. My work specializes in transforming data into 3D visualizations simulating the Earth's surface. Since starting at Boundary RSS, I have learned to synthesize academic research and national databases to expand my technological knowledge.

In addition, I volunteer as a means to use my skills to give back to my community, particularly with organizations aiming to promote environmentally-conscious practices. The Good Loop is a clothing swap where people can bring in unwanted clothing and in turn take home new items, diverting clothing from landfills and distributing goods to charities in need. Stand Up To Trash organizes beach clean-ups, and I have previously volunteered with their events, even giving a lecture to educate other volunteers about effects of climate change on bodies of water.

I take pride in having distinct yet defined career interests. Not necessarily a jack of all trades or a master of one, but somewhere in between, with just the right amount of niches to diversify a set of loosely-connected interests to carve a unique path. My interest in Data Science lies in its professional & problem-solving applications, while my interests in Environmental Science and Legal Analysis lie in personal passion & ambition.
